Welcome to Outlaws to Advocates:

A Journey of Transformation, Hope, and Empowerment

At Outlaws to Advocates, we believe that your past does not define your future. Our mission is to inspire, empower, and support individuals who have faced adversity, helping them transform their lives and reclaim their potential. This platform is more than just a website; it's a community—a place where stories of resilience and change come to life, where we celebrate the strength of the human spirit and the power of redemption.

Our journey from outlaws to advocates is a testament to the belief that anyone can rewrite their story. We've been where you are. We've faced the darkness, the challenges, and the setbacks. But we've also discovered the light, the hope, and the opportunities that come with change. Now, we're here to share our experiences, our knowledge, and our passion for helping others on their own paths to recovery and personal growth.

Meet the Advocates

George Nolan

George Nolan is a living example of how dedication and perseverance can turn a life around. Having been incarcerated multiple times, George knows firsthand the challenges that come with re-entry into society. Instead of letting his past dictate his future, George chose to transform his life. Today, he is a passionate advocate for those in recovery, dedicating his life to helping others find the strength to overcome their own struggles. George's experience, combined with his unyielding commitment to personal growth and community support, makes him a powerful voice for change.

Jesse Messer

Jesse Messer is a dynamic force in the world of recovery and advocacy. Having faced his share of battles, both within himself and in a system that often seems unforgiving, Jesse has emerged not just as a survivor, but as a leader. He is driven by the belief that everyone deserves a second chance and works tirelessly to provide others with the tools and support they need to rebuild their lives. His story of redemption serves as a powerful inspiration for others to believe in their own capacity for change.

Jeremy Schall

Jeremy Schall brings a unique perspective to Outlaws to Advocates, drawing from his own experiences of overcoming adversity. Jeremy’s journey through the challenges of incarceration and recovery has shaped him into a steadfast advocate for those who are often overlooked. His dedication to helping others navigate their paths to recovery is rooted in a deep understanding of the struggles they face. Jeremy’s unwavering commitment to advocacy and empowerment makes him an essential part of our mission.

Our Vision

Outlaws to Advocates is a platform built on the foundations of truth, transparency, and transformation. We understand the complexities of recovery because we've lived them. Our goal is to create a space where individuals can find resources, connect with a supportive community, and gain the inspiration needed to take the next step in their journey.

Whether you're someone who has faced incarceration, struggled with addiction, or simply needs a fresh start, we're here for you. Our stories, our experiences, and our commitment are all geared toward helping you discover your own path to advocacy—because your voice matters, and your story can make a difference.

Thank you for being part of this movement. Together, we can turn our past struggles into future successes. Welcome to Outlaws to Advocates, where we turn adversity into advocacy, one story at a time.
